About us


The Data Science Lab is a high performance research group in Foundations and Applications of Data Science, at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (Madrid, Spain).

The centre pursues three main objectives:

  • Generating new knowlege related to Intelligent Information Technologies (IIT).
  • Cooperation in the analysis of socio-economic problems that require research.
  • Training of young researchers.
